We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to request the deletion of your personal data.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated to you through the app or via email.
